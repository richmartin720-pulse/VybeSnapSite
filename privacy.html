Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

VybeSnap is a friends-only daily vibe sharing app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use VybeSnap:

  • Account information, such as email address, username, display name, and profile details.
  • Content you upload, such as vibe photos, emojis, captions, and reactions.
  • Friend connections, friend requests, and accepted friends.
  • Basic app usage information, such as streak activity and feature usage.
  • Device and technical information needed to operate and secure the app.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Show your vibe posts to your accepted friends.
  • Power features such as streaks, the feed, reactions, and the Vibe Meter.
  • Keep the app safe, prevent abuse, and review reported content.
  • Improve app reliability and user experience.

Friends-Only Sharing

VybeSnap is designed to be private by default. Your vibe posts are intended to be visible only to accepted friends inside the app, unless a future feature clearly states otherwise.

Photos and Uploaded Content

Photos and other uploaded content are stored securely using our backend service providers. You are responsible for the content you upload and should only share content you are comfortable sharing with your accepted friends.

Content Reports and Safety

Users may report content or accounts that violate our rules. Reported content may be hidden, removed, or reviewed. Accounts that repeatedly violate our rules may be restricted or removed.

Third-Party Services

VybeSnap may use trusted third-party services for authentication, database hosting, file storage, analytics, crash reporting, and app infrastructure. These services process data only as needed to operate the app.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell your personal information.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data. See our Delete Account page for instructions.

Children and Young Users

VybeSnap is intended for users who are old enough to use social apps under the laws of their country. Users should not upload harmful, abusive, sexual, illegal, or exploitative content.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@vybesnap.app
