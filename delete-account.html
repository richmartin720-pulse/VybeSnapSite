Delete Account

Use this page to request deletion of your VybeSnap account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account, email us from the email address linked to your VybeSnap account.

Send your request to: support@vybesnap.app

Request Account Deletion

What to Include

Please include:

  • The email address linked to your VybeSnap account.
  • Your username, if known.
  • A clear message saying you want your account deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we aim to delete or anonymise:

  • Your profile information.
  • Your uploaded vibe posts and photos.
  • Your friend connections and account-related data.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

Some limited information may be retained for a short period if required for safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, backups, or dispute handling.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.

In-App Deletion

If account deletion is available inside the app, you may also delete your account from the app settings.
